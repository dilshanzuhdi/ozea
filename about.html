About Ozea

Ozea is a small ceramics brand creating creative vases, cups, and pet accessories in limited runs. Each piece is made in small batches — refined, functional, and designed to elevate everyday spaces.

Design approach
Clean forms, premium presence

Minimal lines, calm neutrals, and sculptural shapes that feel like art — but live well in real homes.

Materials
Small-batch variation

Handmade pieces will naturally vary slightly in glaze and texture — that’s part of their uniqueness.
